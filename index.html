Privacy Policy - BrideToBe

Last updated: June 16, 2025

Welcome to BrideToBe! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when using our application.

1. About BrideToBe

BrideToBe is a mobile platform that provides rental services for wedding attire and accessories such as kebaya, gowns, suits, beskap, and more. Users can browse, select, and rent wedding outfits directly from the app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number.
  • Order Information: Items rented, rental dates, delivery address.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may access your location to recommend nearby rental providers.
  • Usage Data: Device information, operating system, and interaction logs for analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and payments.
  • To provide customer support and technical assistance.
  • To send notifications related to your orders or account.
  • To improve user experience and application performance.
  • For analytics and service improvements.

4. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to securing your data. Your information is stored on secure servers and protected by encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data only when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Fulfill your orders (e.g., with delivery partners or rental vendors).
  • Protect the safety and rights of users and the platform.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Control permissions such as access to location services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be notified via the app or your registered email. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
